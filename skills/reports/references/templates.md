# Report Templates

Copy and customize these templates for different report types.

## Performance Analysis Template

```markdown
# [Topic] Performance Analysis

**Analysis Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Dataset:** [X] records from [source], [date range]
**Method:** [Brief methodology description]

## Executive Summary

[2-3 paragraphs summarizing the key findings and their business implications]

### Key Findings

1. **[Finding 1]**: [Brief description with key metric]
2. **[Finding 2]**: [Brief description with key metric]
3. **[Finding 3]**: [Brief description with key metric]

## Top Performers Analysis

### Sample [Items] Analyzed

| [Item] | Date | [Metric 1] | [Metric 2] | Score |
|--------|------|------------|------------|-------|
| [Name] | [Date] | [Value] | [Value] | [Score] |
| [Name] | [Date] | [Value] | [Value] | [Score] |

### Common Characteristics

#### 1. [Characteristic Category]
- [Specific observation]
- [Supporting detail]

#### 2. [Characteristic Category]
- [Specific observation]
- [Supporting detail]

### Top Performer Example: [Name]
- **[Element 1]**: [Description]
- **[Element 2]**: [Description]
- **Result**: [Metrics]

## Bottom Performers Analysis

### Sample [Items] Analyzed

| [Item] | Date | [Metric 1] | [Metric 2] | Score |
|--------|------|------------|------------|-------|
| [Name] | [Date] | [Value] | [Value] | [Score] |
| [Name] | [Date] | [Value] | [Value] | [Score] |

### Common Characteristics

#### 1. [Characteristic Category]
- [Specific observation]
- [Supporting detail]

### Bottom Performer Example: [Name]
- **[Element 1]**: [Description]
- **[Element 2]**: [Description]
- **Result**: [Metrics]

## What Distinguishes Top from Bottom Performers

| Factor | Top Performers | Bottom Performers |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| [Factor 1] | [Description] | [Description] |
| [Factor 2] | [Description] | [Description] |
| [Factor 3] | [Description] | [Description] |

## Recommendations

### For [Desired Outcome]

1. **[Recommendation 1]** - [Why and how]
2. **[Recommendation 2]** - [Why and how]
3. **[Recommendation 3]** - [Why and how]

### [Conditional Recommendations]

[Specific advice for specific situations]

## Methodology

### Data Source
- [Source description]
- [How data was collected]

### Analysis Method
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

### Limitations
- [Limitation 1]
- [Limitation 2]

## Appendix

### [Full Data List 1]

[Detailed list or table]
```

## Quick Summary Template

For shorter, rapid analysis:

```markdown
# [Topic] Summary

**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Scope:** [What was analyzed]

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value | Benchmark | Status |
|--------|-------|-----------|--------|
| [Metric] | [Value] | [Benchmark] | [Up/Down/Stable] |

## Top Insights

1. **[Insight]**: [1-2 sentence explanation]
2. **[Insight]**: [1-2 sentence explanation]
3. **[Insight]**: [1-2 sentence explanation]

## Action Items

- [ ] [Action 1]
- [ ] [Action 2]
- [ ] [Action 3]

## Next Steps

[What should happen next]
```

## Trend Analysis Template

```markdown
# [Topic] Trend Analysis

**Period:** [Start Date] to [End Date]
**Frequency:** [Daily/Weekly/Monthly]

## Overview

[Brief description of what's being tracked]

## Trend Summary

| Period | [Metric 1] | [Metric 2] | Change |
|--------|------------|------------|--------|
| [Period 1] | [Value] | [Value] | [%] |
| [Period 2] | [Value] | [Value] | [%] |

## Key Observations

### [Trend 1]: [Description]

[Explanation with supporting data]

### [Trend 2]: [Description]

[Explanation with supporting data]

## Drivers

What's causing these trends:

1. **[Driver 1]**: [Explanation]
2. **[Driver 2]**: [Explanation]

## Forecast

[What to expect going forward]

## Recommended Actions

1. [Action based on trend]
2. [Action based on trend]
```
